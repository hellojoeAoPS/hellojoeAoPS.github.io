All About Blooket

I love Blooket because...

  • It is fun!!!
  • It contains many different fun and engaging gamemodes!!!
  • It is a mix of many different fun study games!

How Blooket Works

Blooket is a game where you answer questions based on a study set that you or your teacher creates. The questions are repeating, and there are powerups in some game modes. There is Tower of Doom, Tower Defense, and many more game modes that you can check out. Have fun playing Blooket!
